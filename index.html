About JBC Global Finals (China)

Since its debut in China in 2021, the JBC Global Finals (China) have grown exponentially, establishing the country as a vital hub with worldwide impact. Each August, Beijing welcomes nearly 4,000 competitors from more than a dozen countries and regions for this spectacular celebration of robotics.

The event is proudly supported by its strategic partner, the National Speed Skating Oval ('The Ice Ribbon'), the celebrated centerpiece of the 2022 Winter Olympics.
